Welcome


Hello and welcome, my name is Guy Carter-Richardson and currently live in York, North Yorkshire, UK. I am currently studying a PhD within the Nuclear Astrophysics Group at the University of York. I have recently left employment in the British Army where I served for six and half years as an Commissioned Officer in the Royal Signals.


The ongoing PhD is focussed on understanding nuclear formation within stars, specifically CNO breakout reactions.
